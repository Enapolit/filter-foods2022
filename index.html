Currently, much of the 5th Street Turning Basin is used as a parking lot and dumping site. The Gowanus Neighborhood Plan will convert the turning basin into a site that supports low marsh and high marsh ecologies. The site will include spaces for public recreation and art installations. Additionally, it will connect parts of Gowanus to the Old Stone House and Washington Park.
